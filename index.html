About Me

Bridging biology
and intelligence

"I believe the integration of biology and artificial intelligence has the potential to drive the next generation of medical breakthroughs."

I am an M.Sc student specialising in Data Science and Bio AI at Digital University Kerala, with a background in B.Sc Botany and Biotechnology from Mar Ivanios College. My academic path is shaped by a deep curiosity about how technology can help us understand and improve the natural world.

My focus lies in applying computational and data-driven methods to complex biological challenges — particularly in machine learning-assisted drug discovery, disease prediction, and personalised medicine. I enjoy uncovering meaningful patterns from large datasets and translating them into actionable insights for healthcare.

I aspire to bridge the gap between computer science and laboratory research, and to be part of interdisciplinary efforts that drive genuine innovation in global health.
